Shipping & Returns

We are proud to offer quick and reliable shipping across all of Tunisia. Shipping rates are calculated based on the total value of your order.

  • **Standard Shipping Rate:** A flat rate of **8 DT** applies to all orders under the free shipping minimum.
  • **Free Shipping:** Enjoy **FREE shipping** on all orders of **150 DT and above**.

We typically process orders within 1–2 business days. Delivery usually takes an additional 3–5 business days, depending on your location. You will receive a tracking number once your order is processed and shipped.

Please note that delivery times are based on the time of shipment, not when the order is placed.

Returns & Exchanges Policy

At **Zahrat Al Hayat**, your satisfaction is our priority. We accept returns and exchanges on items that meet the following criteria:

  • Returns must be requested within **14 days** of receiving your order.
  • Items must be returned in **original condition**: unworn, unwashed, with all original tags attached.

**FINAL SALE:** Due to hygiene standards and quality assurance, we **DO NOT accept returns or exchanges** on items that have been opened, used, worn, washed, or altered. Please ensure items are unused before removing any tags.

To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer support team at hello@zahrat-al-hayat.com with your order number.

Need assistance? Contact us Monday–Friday, 10am–3pm.
